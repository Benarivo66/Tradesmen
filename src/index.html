Tips for both Customers and Tradesmen

  • Always check customer reviews and ratings before hiring a tradesman. Look for professionals with consistent positive feedback to ensure quality and reliability!
  • Avoid pouring grease, coffee grounds, or food scraps down the drain. Use a drain guard to catch debris
  • Replace traditional bulbs with LEDs to save energy and reduce long-term costs

  • Clean walls, fill cracks, and sand rough areas for a smooth, professional finish
